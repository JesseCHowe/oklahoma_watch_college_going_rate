College Going Rates: Largest Districts vs. Epic

Among Epic Charter Schools' more than 2,500 graduates in spring 2019, about 15% enrolled as freshmen in fall 2019 in an Oklahoma public college or university. Epic's rate was lower than those for the state's 10 largest school districts. Total 2019 graduates for each district are preliminary.
